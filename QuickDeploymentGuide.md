# Quick Deployment Guide (Mobile-Friendly)

## Step 1: Upload to GitHub
1. Go to [github.com](https://github.com) and log in (or create an account).
2. Tap the "+" icon at the top right > "New repository".
3. Name it something like `scottbaylis-site`, then tap "Create repository".
4. Scroll down to "uploading an existing file".
5. Tap "Choose your files", select the extracted website files from Files app.
6. Commit the changes at the bottom.

## Step 2: Deploy to Vercel
1. Go to [vercel.com](https://vercel.com) and sign in with GitHub.
2. Tap "Add New" > "Project".
3. Choose the repo you just created.
4. Vercel will auto-detect it's a static site — just tap "Deploy".

## Step 3: Connect Domain
1. In your Vercel dashboard, go to your project > Settings > Domains.
2. Tap "Add Domain" and enter `scottbaylis.co.uk`.
3. Follow Vercel’s instructions to update DNS settings in your domain provider.

You're live!
